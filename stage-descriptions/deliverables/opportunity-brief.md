## What this deliverable is for

The Opportunity Brief is the deliverable used to clarify why a topic matters, what value it could create, and whether it should move forward.

It is used in the Early Shaping phase, covering [Value Proposition](stage.html?s=value-proposition) and the early part of [Product Discovery](stage.html?s=product-discovery). Its purpose is to create enough shared understanding to support a decision about whether to invest more time and effort.

The Opportunity Brief should stay lightweight. It is not a business case or a delivery plan. It is the minimum description needed to frame the opportunity clearly and support a prioritisation decision.
