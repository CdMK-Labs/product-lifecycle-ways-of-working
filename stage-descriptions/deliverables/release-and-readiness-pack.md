## What this deliverable is for

The Release & Readiness Pack is the deliverable used to confirm that a change is safe to release, that rollout has been prepared, that support readiness is in place, and that go-live is under control.

It is used in [Deploy](stage.html?s=deploy). Its purpose is to ensure that the transition from development into live use is handled responsibly, with the right checks, the right people informed, and the right support in place.

The Release & Readiness Pack is not a checklist for its own sake. It is the confirmation that the product is ready to move into live operation and that the organisation is ready to support it.
