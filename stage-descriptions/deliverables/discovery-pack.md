## What this deliverable is for

The Discovery Pack is the deliverable used to capture what was learned during discovery, which options were explored, what risks and dependencies matter, and whether the topic is ready for planning and approval.

It is the output of [Product Discovery](stage.html?s=product-discovery). Its purpose is to reduce uncertainty enough to make a responsible move into the planning and commitment stage.

The Discovery Pack is not a full business case. It is the evidence and structured thinking that feeds into the [Business Case & Delivery Decision Pack](deliverable.html?d=business-case-and-delivery-decision-pack), which is where the formal commitment and approval case is made.
