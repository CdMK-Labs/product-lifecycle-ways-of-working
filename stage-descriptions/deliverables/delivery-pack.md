## What this deliverable is for

The Delivery Pack is the practical execution deliverable used to track scope, progress, dependencies, quality and readiness for release.

It is used throughout [Develop & Deliver](stage.html?s=develop-and-deliver). Its purpose is to give the team and stakeholders a shared, current view of what is being built, how it is progressing, and whether the work is on track to meet its commitments.

The Delivery Pack is not a static plan. It should evolve as the work moves forward, reflecting what has been delivered, what remains, and what has changed.
