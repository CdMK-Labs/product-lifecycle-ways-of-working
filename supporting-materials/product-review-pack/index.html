Product Review Pack

About this deliverable

The Product Review Pack is the recurring live-product review deliverable used to support ongoing operations, learning and improvement. It is used across Operate & Monitor, Measure & Learn and Adapt & Optimize.

It includes three standing sections: Operational Health, Measurement & Learning, and Optimization & Lifecycle Decisions.
