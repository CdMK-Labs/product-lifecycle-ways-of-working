Product Review Pack

What this pack is for

The recurring deliverable for live-product review in Operations. Covers service health, usage and adoption signals, and improvement decisions across three stages: Operate & Monitor, Measure & Learn and Adapt & Optimize.

Download starter pack (.zip)

Includes a main pack placeholder. Starter files will be expanded in future versions.

What's in this pack

  • 01-Product-Review-Pack.docx — main pack. Three standing sections: Operational Health (service health and operational signals), Measurement & Learning (usage, adoption and outcome signals) and Optimization & Lifecycle Decisions (improvement needs and product choices). Every review ends with agreed decisions and actions with owners and dates.

When to use this pack

Use throughout Operations, whenever the product is live and in active use. Run on a regular cadence: monthly, quarterly or as fits the product and governance context.

  • the team and business stakeholders need a shared, recurring view of how the product is performing
  • issues, signals or decisions need to be surfaced and acted on together
  • improvement priorities need to be set, reviewed or updated
