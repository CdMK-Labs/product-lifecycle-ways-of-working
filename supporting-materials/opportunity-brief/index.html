Opportunity Brief

What this pack is for

A short document used at the start of the lifecycle to frame a problem or opportunity and support a next-step decision.

Not a business case, not a delivery plan, and not a solution design.

Download starter pack (.zip)

Includes a main pack placeholder. Starter files will be expanded in future versions.

What's in this pack

  • 01-Opportunity-Brief.docx — main pack. Covers the problem or opportunity, why it matters now, who is affected, expected value, strategic fit and a clear recommendation.

When to use this pack

Use in Value Proposition, when a new opportunity or problem has surfaced and needs framing before any further investment.

  • a new business need or opportunity has surfaced and needs structured framing
  • a team needs a shared view of why something matters before deciding whether to take it into discovery
  • a prioritisation conversation is coming up and the topic needs to be visible
