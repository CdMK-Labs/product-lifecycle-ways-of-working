Release & Readiness Pack

What this pack is for

Supports safe release, controlled rollout and a smooth move into live operation. It brings together the most important release and readiness information so the team and stakeholders can see whether the change is genuinely ready to release, support and use.

It is not only a technical deployment checklist. The outcome is either a confident move to live, or a clear decision to stabilise, rework or rollback.

What's in this pack

  • 01-Release-and-Readiness-Pack.docx — main pack. Covers the release summary, deployment plan, rollout plan, readiness checklist, support setup, monitoring readiness, access and permissions, runbooks and support notes, communication and training actions, rollback plan, go-live issues and a clear go-live confirmation or decision.

When to use this pack

Use in Deploy, when the relevant changes are ready to move toward production and the team needs to confirm that deployment, support and user readiness have been handled.

  • quality, security and non-functional checks have reached the level needed for go-live
  • support, monitoring and operational readiness need to be confirmed
  • rollout, communication and training activities need to be coordinated
  • the team needs a clear basis for the go-live decision
