Business Case & Delivery Decision Pack

About this deliverable

The Business Case & Delivery Decision Pack is the main decision deliverable for approval and commitment. It is used in the Align & Plan stage to bring together value case and cost case, and secure the required approval.

It is supported by four appendices: KPI Appendix, Architecture Appendix, Estimates & Financial Appendix, and Risk Appendix.
