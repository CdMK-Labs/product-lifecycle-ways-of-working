Business Case & Delivery Decision Pack

What this pack is for

The approval deliverable for the Development stage. It brings together value case, cost case and delivery plan so decision-makers can commit with confidence, and the delivery team has a clear baseline for execution.

Use it when a topic has moved beyond discovery and needs formal commitment or sign-off before significant execution begins.

Download starter pack (.zip)

Includes a main pack and four appendix placeholders. Starter files will be expanded in future versions.

What's in this pack

  • 01-Business-Case-and-Delivery-Decision-Pack.docx — main pack. The decision narrative: opportunity, value case, scope, delivery setup, timeline, cost summary, key risks and the decision requested. This is what decision-makers read.
  • Appendix-KPI.docx — KPI framework, how success will be measured, ownership and reporting cadence.
  • Appendix-Architecture.docx — high-level technical direction, key integrations and major architectural constraints.
  • Appendix-Estimates-and-Financial.docx — development cost, run-cost implications, assumptions and contingency.
  • Appendix-Risk.docx — major risks, mitigation actions, dependencies and confidence level.

The main pack should be readable without the appendices. Not every commitment needs all four appendices developed in full — scale the depth to the size of the decision.

When to use this pack

Use in Align & Plan, when Product Discovery has created enough clarity to move from exploration into commitment.

  • an initiative requires formal financial commitment or approval
  • a smaller product change needs a clear commitment conversation within normal product or portfolio governance
  • the business owner, product team and delivery team need alignment before execution starts
