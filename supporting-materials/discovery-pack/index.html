Discovery Pack

What this pack is for

Captures what was learned during discovery to support a clear next-step decision. It reduces uncertainty before planning and commitment begins.

A learning deliverable, not a planning or approval document. The pack covers problem understanding, options explored, risks and a recommendation: move into Align & Plan, return for reframing, hold, or stop.

What's in this pack

  • 01-Discovery-Pack.docx — main pack. Covers the refined problem statement, user and stakeholder insight, key assumptions and unknowns, options explored, risks and dependencies, evidence from discovery activities, and a clear recommendation.

When to use this pack

Use in Product Discovery, when an opportunity has moved beyond initial framing and the team needs to understand the problem better before committing to a plan.

  • an Opportunity Brief has been prioritised for further exploration
  • stakeholders, users, risks or options need to be understood before a commitment makes sense
  • the team needs to test assumptions and reduce uncertainty before moving into Align & Plan
