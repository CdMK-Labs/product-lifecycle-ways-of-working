Delivery Pack

What this pack is for

The working deliverable for execution. Tracks scope, progress, dependencies, quality and deployment readiness throughout build and delivery.

Not a status report. Keep it close to delivery tools and update it as work progresses.

Download starter pack (.zip)

Includes a main pack placeholder. Starter files will be expanded in future versions.

What's in this pack

  • 01-Delivery-Pack.docx — main pack. Covers current scope and priorities, work breakdown, dependencies and issues, key implementation decisions, quality and test evidence, security and compliance checks, validation outcomes, and open items before deployment.

When to use this pack

Use in Develop & Deliver, once the required approvals are in place and the team is moving from planning into execution.

  • scope, priorities and dependencies need to stay visible during the work
  • quality and operational readiness need to be tracked as part of delivery
  • the team needs a shared view of what remains before deployment
