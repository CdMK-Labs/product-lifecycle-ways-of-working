## Prioritisation Criteria

_Items are scored on the following dimensions:_

- **Impact:** How significantly will this improve user outcomes or key metrics?
- **Effort:** How much time and resource does this require?
- **Confidence:** How confident are we this will work, based on evidence?

_Priority 1 = highest. Items with high impact, low effort, and high confidence should be at the top._
