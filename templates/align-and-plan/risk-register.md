## Risk Scoring Guide

**Likelihood:** 1 = Rare · 2 = Unlikely · 3 = Possible · 4 = Likely · 5 = Almost certain
**Impact:** 1 = Negligible · 2 = Minor · 3 = Moderate · 4 = Significant · 5 = Critical
**Score = Likelihood × Impact** (1–10 = Low · 11–15 = Medium · 16–25 = High)
