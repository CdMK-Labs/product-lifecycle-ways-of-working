## Purpose

The Product Owner is accountable for maximising customer and business value by owning value delivery across the product lifecycle.

## Responsibilities

- Product backlog ownership
- Day-to-day priority decisions
- Stakeholder engagement
- Adoption and feedback loops
- Risk mitigation

## Deliverables

- Prioritised backlog
- Acceptance criteria
- KPIs
- Product roadmap

## Reporting line

Zones and Functions.
