## Purpose

The IT Adoption Specialist drives successful and sustained adoption of IT products by leading change, advocating for the end user, embedding user-centric design practices and applying data-driven insights.

## Responsibilities

- Acting as the voice of the end user
- Tracking and analysing adoption
- Conducting user research
- Translating technical concepts into user-friendly communication
- Creating learning experiences and adoption communities
- Assessing change impact
- Stakeholder collaboration

## Deliverables

- Adoption and readiness assessments
- User research outputs
- Wireframes and prototypes
- Adoption plans
- Communication and training materials
- Adoption metrics and insights

## Measured on

- User adoption
- User satisfaction
- Reduction in how-to tickets
- Training completion
- Productivity and benefit realisation

## Reporting line

Reports to IT Value Partner or Head of Product.
