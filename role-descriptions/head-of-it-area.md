## Purpose

The Head of IT Area holds strategic and operational IT product responsibility for one or more Zones and Functions. The role is end-to-end accountable for translating strategic vision into portfolio outcomes, guiding investment decisions and establishing guardrails that ensure consistency and accountability. It balances speed and flexibility in delivery with stability, security, quality and compliance to support a cohesive product-centric operating model.

## Responsibilities

- Zone and Functions IT and Digital strategy and roadmap, ensuring investment plans are aligned to Zone and Functions goals
- End-to-end accountable for IT Products in the portfolio and escalation point for IT Product Enablement
- Accountable for on-time, on-budget value-adding delivery aligned to security, risk and compliance, cyber risk management, regulatory compliance, vendor and contract management across the IT product lifecycle
- Accountable for driving team performance and deliverables towards tangible outcomes
- Accountable for service delivery and operations, ensuring reliability, availability, business continuity and resilience
- Act as trusted partner to Zone and Functions and the IT Product organisation
- Accountable for the IT budget, Total Cost of Ownership, forecast and cost, and gatekeeper for business cases
- Building and leading a high-performing IT organisation and fostering a culture of psychological safety, accountability and continuous learning

## Deliverables

- IT and Digital strategy and roadmap
- Alignment between IT and Zones and Functions
- Portfolio outcomes
- Capability maturity and improvement
- Reliability and availability, SLA
- IT budget and TCO
- Vendor strategy, renewals and scorecards
- Audit ownership

## Measured on

- Portfolio value
- SLA and KPIs
- Business review
- Delivery predictability
- Compliance: security, audits, IT fundamentals
- TCO and budget
- Delivery performance
- People turnover
- Barometer
- Staff cost

## Collaborates with

- CIO and IT LT
- IT Product Enablement
- Head of Product and Product Architects
- Zone and Functions Stakeholders
- Zone and Functions Product Management and key functional stakeholders
- Strategic vendors and system integrators

## Reporting line

Reports to CIO. As a guiding principle, direct reports to the IT Director are the IT Delivery Partner, IT Value Partner, IT Architecture Partner and IT Portfolio Partner. In exceptional cases, Head of Product.
