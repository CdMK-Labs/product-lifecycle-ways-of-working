## Purpose

The IT Product Architect owns and drives the product architecture in alignment with the Head of IT Product, ensuring it is fit for purpose and that technical debt is kept as low as possible. The IT Product Architect is overall accountable for compliance with Arla IT's standards.

## Responsibilities

- The product architecture and non-functional requirements, including integrations
- Overseeing and maintaining applications and legacy systems, including obligations and plans
- Building and maintaining the technical roadmap for products
- Ensuring the product adheres to security and architecture standards
- Guiding tool and technology selection to ensure alignment with product strategy and architecture standards
- Setting the quality and documentation standard for the product
- Maintaining the technical debt tracker and keeping technical debt as low as possible
- Coaching IT Specialists on architectural matters

## Deliverables

- Technical roadmap
- Technical debt tracker
- Architecture documentation

## Measured on

- Maintaining low and visible technical debt
- Ensuring consistent adherence to security and architectural standards
- Upholding high quality and documentation standards across the product

## Collaborates with

- Head of Product and IT Specialists
- Product Owner and Product Manager
- IT Product Enablement

## Reporting line

Reports to IT Architecture Partner. As a guiding principle, direct reports are IT Specialists.
