Roles & Responsibilities

What this page is for

This page describes the roles that operate across the IT and Digital product organisation. Use it to understand what each role is responsible for, what it delivers and how it connects to the rest of the team.

Roles are grouped by category. Open a role to see the full description.

IT Product Leadership

Head of IT Area

Strategic and operational IT leadership for one or more Zones and Functions.

Head of IT Product

End-to-end accountable for the product lifecycle, delivery outcomes and team.

IT Product Architect

Owns product architecture, technical roadmap and compliance with IT standards.

IT Specialists

IT Specialist

Delivers medium to complex technical and functional solutions.

IT Developer

Delivers working software increments and maintains code quality and system reliability.

IT Delivery Lead

Facilitates, plans and coordinates product team deliveries and operations.

IT Adoption Specialist

Drives sustained adoption through user research, change management and training.

IT Supporter

Primary point of contact for IT incidents and service requests.

IT Product Enablement

IT Delivery Partner

Servant leader ensuring efficient cross-product delivery and team coaching.

IT Value Partner

Aligns Zones and Functions with IT to translate strategy into value-driven deliveries.

IT Architecture Partner

Sets architectural direction and coaches Product Architects across products.

IT Portfolio Partner

Administrates the IT portfolio with financial control and governance.

IT SC Market Partner

Collaborates with site management on IT operational issues and maintenance planning.

Business Roles

Product Owner

Accountable for maximising customer and business value through backlog ownership.

Product Manager

Shapes and prioritises portfolio backlogs aligned to business strategy.

Business Owner

Owns investments, benefits, outcomes and release decisions.
