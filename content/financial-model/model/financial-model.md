## What this page is for

The Financial Model helps teams understand how funding, approval, forecasting, and reporting connect to product and delivery work.

It is a practical guide for navigating the key financial processes that support decision-making across the lifecycle, from early ideas to execution and follow-up.

Use this section to understand:

- which financial process is relevant
- when to involve the right people
- what is needed to move forward
- how financial follow-up connects to delivery and outcomes

## Core financial processes

The Financial Model is structured around four core financial processes. Each process supports a different part of the journey from idea to follow-up.

- **01 [allocate-funding-frames] Funding Frame Allocation** — Defines the financial frame available for planned work and helps create transparency on funding boundaries and priorities.
- **02 [approve-investments] Approve Investment** — Moves capex investments through staged approval gates from early shaping to funded delivery and later benefit follow-up.
- **03 [forecast-financials] Financial Forecasting** — Helps teams and stakeholders maintain an updated view of expected spend and financial development over time.
- **04 [report-financial-status] Financial Reporting** — Provides follow-up on actuals, financial performance, and relevant reporting to support transparency and governance.
