Develop & Deliver is where the product increment is built, tested, and made ready for release. Teams work iteratively, maintaining quality and transparency throughout the delivery cycle.

## Entry Criteria

- Delivery plan is approved and scope is locked for the increment
- Technical approach and architecture decisions are documented
- Definition of Done is agreed by the team

## Deliverables

- Completed and tested product increment meeting the Definition of Done
- Updated documentation and runbooks
- Release candidate ready for deployment
- Stakeholder sign-off on the increment
