## Purpose

The IT Delivery Partner is a servant leader and coach ensuring efficient cross-product delivery and acting as the escalation point for delivery issues.

## Responsibilities

- Demand and capacity management
- Supplier collaboration
- Cross-product delivery
- Quality assurance
- Risk and issue management
- Continuous improvement

## Deliverables

- Delivery progress and value tracking
- Risk mitigation
- Quality-assured forecasts and estimates

## Reporting line

Reports to IT LT.
