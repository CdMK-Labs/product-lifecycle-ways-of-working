## Purpose

The Head of IT Product is accountable for maximising the value of their product to the receiving business or stakeholders. The role holds end-to-end accountability for the product lifecycle and for building a strong, high-performing team. While focused on delivering business outcomes, the Head of IT Product must also ensure that the product complies with Arla IT's non-functional requirements and adheres to the Fundamentals in Arla IT. The role balances speed and flexibility with stability and compliance, ensuring the product meets business needs while remaining sustainable and secure in the long term.

## Responsibilities

- Overall responsible for the IT Product lifecycle, from product strategy, maintenance and operations through development to successful decommission
- Responsible for all deliverables, including various demands and operations
- Delivery according to business outcome targets, SLAs and business KPIs
- Responsible for ensuring the backlog is prioritised by the Product Owner
- Compliance with Arla's IT governance, security, reliability and data privacy policies
- Leads the team and ensures the right staffing and team composition
- Develops a high-performing team
- Team culture and health
- Management of third-party vendors

## Deliverables

- Product strategy
- Business outcome
- Risk mitigations
- Continuous improvements
- Roadmaps
- Investment estimation
- Forecast
- Delivery according to value targets, SLA and KPIs

## Measured on

- Value
- Progress
- Operations
- KPIs
- Service Level Agreements
- Continuous improvements
- Barometer results

## Collaborates with

- Product Owner
- IT Product Enablement
- Other Heads of Product
- Zone and Functions end users

## Reporting line

Reports to IT Delivery Partner or IT LT. As a guiding principle, direct reports to Head of Product are IT Specialist, Adoption Specialist and IT Delivery Lead.
