## Purpose

The IT Delivery Lead manages deliveries and operations according to agreed expectations and is responsible for the proactive facilitation, planning and coordination of product team deliverables.

## Responsibilities

- Facilitation, planning and coordination of deliverables
- Ensuring delivery against agreed business outcome targets, SLAs and KPIs
- Managing external service contracts
- Managing staff augmentation contracts
- Forecasting, purchase orders and service orders
- Applying fit-for-purpose delivery methods

## Deliverables

- Delivery according to agreements
- Business outcome targets and SLAs
- Risk mitigation
- Forecasts

## Measured on

- Forecast accuracy
- Efficiencies
- Delivery KPIs
- Service levels

## Reporting line

Reports to Head of Product or IT Delivery Partner.
