## Purpose

The IT Specialist works on medium to complex tasks using specialised IT expertise to deliver effective technical and functional solutions. The role translates business needs into practical designs, implementations and support, and delivers reliable, high-quality IT outcomes.

## Responsibilities

- Maturing, designing, planning and estimating deliverables
- Building, configuring or implementing deliverables aligned to functional requirements
- Supporting and maintaining according to SLAs
- Investigating issues with IT Products and processes
- Ensuring alignment with Arla's IT Product Lifecycle Fundamentals standards

## Deliverables

- Deliverable estimates
- Solution proposals
- Implemented technical or functional solutions

## Measured on

- Delivery KPIs
- Service levels
- Continuous improvements

## Collaborates with

- IT Specialists
- IT Delivery Lead
- Product Architect
- Head of Product
- Zone and Functions stakeholders

## Reporting line

Reports to Head of Product, Product Architect or IT Delivery Lead.
