## Purpose

The IT Supporter serves as the primary point of contact for IT and data-related incidents and service requests, providing end-to-end support following established processes.

## Responsibilities

- Multi-level support
- Troubleshooting and user guidance
- Incident and request tracking
- Master data maintenance
- Operational reporting
- Process improvement

## Deliverables

- Resolved incidents and requests
- Master data changes
- Operational reports
- Knowledge articles

## Measured on

- SLA adherence
- Incident reduction
- Customer satisfaction
- Data quality metrics

## Reporting line

Reports to IT Delivery Lead or Head of Product.
