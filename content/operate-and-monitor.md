## Purpose
Ensure stable and secure operations.

## Entry Criteria
- Solution deployed to production
- Monitoring and support setup in place

## What Happens
- The product team maintains operational ownership — you build it, you run it — rather than handing off to a separate operations function
- Dashboards and alerts provide real-time visibility into system health, performance, availability, and error rates
- Incidents are managed with clear ownership: a defined response process, active communication, and post-mortems to prevent recurrence
- Routine operational reviews are held to assess SLA/SLO performance, surface recurring issues, and track improvement actions
- Runbooks, on-call guides, and escalation paths are actively maintained by the team and kept current

## Deliverables
- Stable operations (SLA/SLO met)
- Incidents resolved within targets
- Operational insights captured

## Who Is Involved
- Product Team
- IT Supporter
- Delivery Lead
