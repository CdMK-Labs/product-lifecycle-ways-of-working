Product Discovery involves understanding user needs, validating assumptions, and exploring the solution space. Teams use research, prototyping, and experimentation to reduce uncertainty before committing to building.

## Entry Criteria

- Value proposition is defined and approved
- Research scope and goals are agreed
- Access to users or relevant data sources is confirmed

## Deliverables

- User research findings and insights
- Validated or invalidated key assumptions
- Proposed solution direction or concept
- Recommendation to proceed, pivot, or stop
