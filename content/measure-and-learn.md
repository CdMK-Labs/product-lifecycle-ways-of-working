## Purpose

## Entry Criteria
- Product in use
- Metrics defined (from Value Proposition)

## What Happens
We track KPIs, adoption, and business impact.

- Measured performance vs. targets
- Insights documented
- Recommendations for next actions

- Value Partner
- Adoption Specialist
- Portfolio Partner
