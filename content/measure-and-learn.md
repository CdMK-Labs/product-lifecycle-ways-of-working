Measure & Learn focuses on evaluating how well the product is achieving its intended outcomes. Teams collect and analyse data, gather user feedback, and derive insights to inform future decisions.

## Entry Criteria

- Success metrics and measurement plan were defined during Value Proposition or Align & Plan
- Sufficient time in production for meaningful data to be available
- Data collection mechanisms are functioning correctly

## Deliverables

- Performance report against defined success metrics
- User feedback synthesis and key themes
- Documented learnings and actionable recommendations
- Input to Adapt & Optimize or future inflow items
